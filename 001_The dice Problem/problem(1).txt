You are given a cubic dice with 6 faces. All the individual faces have a number printed on them. The numbers are in the range of 1 to6, like any ordinary dice. You will be provided with a face of this cube, your task is to guess the number on the opposite face of the cube.
test cases:
 Example 1:

     Input:
          N = 6
    Output:
          1
   Explanation:
     For dice facing number 6 opposite face
          will have the number 1.

 Example 2:

   Input:
       N = 2
  Output:
       5
  Explanation:
      For dice facing number 5 opposite face
         will have the number 2.

Your Task:  
Your task is to complete the function oppositeFaceOfDice() which takes an integer N as an input parameter and return the number on the opposite face of the dice.
